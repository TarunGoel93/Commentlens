About CommentLens

CommentLens harnesses the power of advanced AI to analyze comments from multiple platforms, providing content creators and brands with invaluable insights into audience sentiment, spam detection, and data-driven content strategy recommendations.

Key Features

Multi-Platform Analysis

Analyze comments across YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter in one centralized dashboard.

Sentiment Detection

Identify positive, negative, and neutral sentiments in comments with high accuracy.

Spam & Bot Detection

Automatically filter out spam and bot comments to focus on genuine audience feedback.

Trending Topics

Discover popular keywords and topics in your comments to guide content creation.

Engagement Metrics

Track user engagement and interaction rates to measure content performance.

AI-Powered Suggestions

Receive content strategy recommendations based on comprehensive audience feedback analysis.
